# Takumi Seto

The Takumi-Seto workshop produces high-quality Seto-yaki porcelain in the historic Aichi Prefecture.

![Seto Porcelain](/static/images/heritagefocused_setoyaki_worksho.png)

## The Origin
Seto-yaki is one of Japan's oldest and most prestigious ceramic styles.

## High Standards
Our artisans take great pride in their meticulous attention to detail and traditional firing techniques.
