# Bizen-Yama

The Bizen-Yama workshop is located in Okayama Prefecture. They are famous for their unglazed Bizen ware.

![Bizen Pottery](/static/images/traditional_japanese_bizenyaki_p.png)

## The Tradition
With over 1000 years of history, Bizen pottery is one of Japan's six ancient kilns.

## The Process
The pieces are fired for several days in a wood-burning kiln, which produces unique patterns from the ash.
