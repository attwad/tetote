# Deidei

Deidei focuses on earthy, rustic stoneware for every day use.

![Rustic Pottery](/static/images/detailed_shots_of_handcrafted_ja.png)

## The Philosophy
We believe beauty exists in the everyday objects we use in our homes.

## Wabi-Sabi Aesthetic
Our designs embrace the imperfections of the clay and the unique results of each firing.
